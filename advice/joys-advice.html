Hi my name is Joy and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up!

It's seems overwhelming and complex at first, but once you get going everything starts falling into place. Push through those tough lessons and get over the little humps!

It's ok to stop, take a break and come back to your code when you are stuck. I've found that when I'm really stuck on a problem, I need to step back, do something else or sleep on it. When I come back with a fresh mind I can usually solve the problem quickly.

And don't be afraid to ask for help.

Keep practicing! Keeping looking for inspiration!

And most importantly enjoy learning a new skill and celebrate your accomplishments!
